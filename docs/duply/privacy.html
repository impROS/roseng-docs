Duply — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Duply does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Duply is a disk-cleaning and storage utility that runs entirely on your Mac inside Apple's App Sandbox.

What Duply accesses

  • Only the folders you grant. Duply can read and clean files only in locations you explicitly choose through the macOS system file picker (for example, your Home folder so it can find rebuildable caches, or /Applications when you uninstall an app). Access is stored as a security-scoped bookmark on your device.
  • Duply reads file metadata (sizes, dates, app bundle identifiers) locally to show you what can be removed.

What Duply does NOT do

  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry.
  • No accounts, no login, no cloud.
  • Your file names, contents, and index never leave your Mac.

Data deletion

When you clean items, Duply either moves them to the Trash (recoverable from Finder) or deletes them permanently — according to the option you choose. Protected and system-sensitive locations are never touched.
