Duply

Clean junk, free your disk.

Duply finds rebuildable caches, junk and big files on your Mac, shows you exactly what would be removed, and lets you clean with one click — privately and on-device.

Frequently asked questions

Why does Duply ask for access to my Home folder?

Duply is sandboxed by macOS, so it can only see folders you allow. Granting your Home folder lets it scan rebuildable caches under ~/Library. Nothing leaves your Mac. You can change this anytime in the Clean screen.

Is it safe? Will I lose anything important?

Duply only removes rebuildable caches, logs, and the items you select. You can expand any category down to the file level before cleaning, and choose “Move to Trash” (recoverable) instead of permanent deletion. System-sensitive locations (Keychains, SSH keys, etc.) are never touched.

Why couldn't Duply remove an app from /Applications?

The sandbox requires your explicit permission to delete from /Applications. When prompted, grant access in the system panel and the uninstall completes. Leftover files are cleaned with your Home access.

How do I change the language?

Settings → General → Language (System / English / Türkçe). The app restarts to apply the new language.
