How do I play?

Read the meaning of the word on your box, then steer into the box painted in that color. The text is there to fool you — trust the word, not the color.

How do I control it?

Tap left/right (or use the on-screen lanes) to switch lanes. One wrong lane ends the run.

Sound, haptics & reset

Open Settings in the app to toggle sound and haptics, replay the tutorial, or reset ad consent.

Is my data safe?

Yes. No login, no personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
